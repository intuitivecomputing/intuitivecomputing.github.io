About the Lab

Welcome to the Intuitive Computing Laboratory at Johns Hopkins University. Our research lab is an interdisciplinary team affiliated with the Department of Computer Science, Malone Center for Engineering in Healthcare, and Laboratory for Computational Sensing and Robotics. Our research focuses on human-robot interaction, human-computer interaction, human-ai teaming , and assistive technology.
