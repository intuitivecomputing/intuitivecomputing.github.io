About the Lab

Aliquam ut ex ut interdum donec amet imperdiet eleifend

Welcome to the Intuitive Computing Laboratory at Johns Hopkins University. Our research lab is an interdisciplinary team affiliated with the Department of Computer Science, Malone Center for Engineering in Healthcare, and Laboratory for Computational Sensing+Robotics. Our research focuses on human-robot interaction, human-computer interaction, human-machine teamwork, and assistive technology.
